**Globus** is the only permitted data-transfer protocol for highly sensitive data.

**UVA IVY-DTN** is the the official collection for moving files into High-Security Research Standard Storage.

To transfer data to High-Security Research Standard Storage, please see the special Globus instructions [here on the RC website](https://www.rc.virginia.edu/userinfo/ivy/ "The RC website's Ivy page").

{{< warning >}}
Ensure that you are  __NOT__ connected to the HSVPN. Data transfer will not work if you are connected to the HSVPN.
{{< /warning >}}

Additional instructions and information on using Globus can be found [here on the RC learning site](https://learning.rc.virginia.edu/tutorials/globus-data-transfer/ "RC Learning's Globus tutorial").
